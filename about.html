WWise

BTM:R - Dialogue - Organization

Each of the character's dialogue is seperated into folders to easily find each dialogue line incase of issues / adjustments.

BTM:R - Acidic Prime - Sound Library

The sound for each stack of Acidic Prime, goes through a scale of pitches to indicate the power. The number of stacks an enemy has is saved in game and is sent to Wwise to play the correct sound corresponding to the number of stacks.

BeatTheMachine:R - Boss Sounds

Boss Sounds are seperated into folders to keep them organized. Sounds are played when bosses enter 'phases'. Sounds stay active till a new 'phase' is entered or until end of animation.

BeatTheMachine:R - Music

Each Music Soundtrack is broken up into each instrument so we have the ability to dynamically change it based on area's entered. Entering a difficult room? Let's add some bass!
