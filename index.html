ABOUT ME

My passion for game development started as a child when I played games on the Sega Genesis with my father. I always wondered how these games were made. This passion I found ended up turning into a life career for me, and it made me think about what I wished to do with my life. Below you can find a copy of my résumé and the series of different works I have done related to game development, programming, and audio.

Games:

BEAT THE MACHINE: REBOOTED

Project Lead/Owner of this startup Steam title. The Beta of this game was launched in July of 2020, with the full game releasing in April of 2022. I was responsible for the Soundtrack, SFX, User Interface and User Experience.

LEGENDS AND WARFARE

A MOBA style game, that launched at PAX EAST 2019. Worked on the networking code, and programmed all 15 character abilites along with the username, password functionalities and character data.

WWise:

BTM:R - Dialogue - Organization

Each of the character's dialogue is seperated into folders to easily find each dialogue line incase of issues / adjustments.

BTM:R - Acidic Prime - Sound Library

The sound for each stack of Acidic Prime, goes through a scale of pitches to indicate the power. The number of stacks an enemy has is saved in game and is sent to Wwise to play the correct sound corresponding to the number of stacks.

BTM:R - Boss Sounds

Boss Sounds are seperated into folders to keep them organized. Sounds are played when bosses enter 'phases'. Sounds stay active till a new 'phase' is entered or until end of animation.

BTM:R - Music

Each Music Soundtrack is broken up into each instrument so we have the ability to dynamically change it based on area's entered. Entering a difficult room? Let's add some bass!
